Pest Identification Guide

Adristyrannus

A type of predatory bug that can sometimes be mistaken for harmful pests. Generally beneficial but can occasionally damage plants.

Medium Risk Predator
Aphids

Small, soft-bodied insects that feed on plant sap. Cause yellowing, wilting, and stunted growth. Reproduce rapidly.

High Risk Sap Feeder
Beetle

Hard-bodied insects that can be both beneficial and harmful. Some species eat other pests, while others damage crops.

Variable Risk Chewer
Bugs

General category of true bugs. Many are beneficial predators, but some can damage plants by piercing and sucking.

Low-Medium Risk Mixed
Mites

Tiny arachnids that cause stippling, yellowing, and webbing on leaves. Thrive in hot, dry conditions.

Medium-High Risk Leaf Damage
Weevil

Beetles with elongated snouts. Both adults and larvae can cause significant damage to crops, especially grains and fruits.

High Risk Borer
